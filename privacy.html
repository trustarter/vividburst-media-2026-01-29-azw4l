Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize you, remember your preferences, and understand how you use our Services. Cookies can be either "persistent" (remaining on your device until deleted) or "session-based" (deleted when you close your browser).

Types of Cookies We Use

  • check_circle

    Essential Cookies

    Required for the website to function properly, including security and authentication purposes.

  • check_circle

    Analytical Cookies

    Help us understand how visitors use our website and how to improve our Services.

  • check_circle

    Marketing Cookies

    Used to deliver targeted advertising and track the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.

  • check_circle

    Preference Cookies

    Remember your preferences and settings to provide a personalized experience.

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party service providers, including Google Analytics and advertising partners, to place cookies on your device. These third parties may collect information about your online activities across different websites for analytics, advertising, and other purposes. You can control third-party cookies through your browser settings or opt out through industry opt-out mechanisms.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. You can also delete cookies from your device at any time. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website and your ability to access certain features.

To learn more about cookies and how to manage them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org or www.youronlinechoices.com.

Your Privacy Rights

You may request that we correct inaccurate information or delete information that is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.

You may opt out of receiving promotional emails and marketing communications by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails or contacting us directly.
